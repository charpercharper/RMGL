COMBINED SHIPPING & SHIPPING SPEED

Combined Shipping is easy! Our items are all in the system by weight. Put the items you'd like into the eBay shopping cart and they'll calculate automatically. We sell art supplies, not postage and handling. Our postage rates are based on the actual weight and size of your order.

There's no such thing as free shipping. Free shipping is just postage costs added to the item price and that just doesn't work for our loyal buyers. If you buy more than one item (as most buyers do), you'll pay much less by paying postage one time than by having it added to each item under a "free" shipping gimmick.

We do, however, have some amazing shipping promotions throughout our store where we'll pitch in on the postage costs.

Shipping Speed: We're quick shippers. All items are in stock and ready to ship, many the same day. We ship Monday through Friday and if we're in the studio, we'll ship on Saturdays, just for you!

RETURNS

Change your mind? No problem. We accept returns for any reason. Use eBay's return hub to return your item. It's easy. Please pack the item securely and return it to us, unused, unopened and undamaged within 30 days.
